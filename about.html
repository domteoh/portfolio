Hello.

I'm a videographer, editor and 2D animator. Whether it's documentaries, music videos or skate videos, I enjoy bringing stories to life — all the more when it's something meaningful.

I'm also a video producer with BFM, where I get to work on a range of shows, from short-form social media content to long-form video podcasts, covering such topics as current events and personal finance.

When I'm not working, you might find me skating the streets of Kuala Lumpur or exploring ideas at Subscript — a dumping ground for thoughts, but mostly a self-indulgent attempt to exercise my brain. Thoughts floating in my head lately revolve around public space, skateboarding and media studies.
